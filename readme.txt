== Description ==

Plugin "Funny video online" displays Funny video on your blog. There are over 10,000 video clips.
Add Funny YouTube videos to your sidebar on your blog using  a widget. Video are saved on our database, so you don't need to have space for all that information. 

<b>Try the Demo</b>

If you're interested in seeing what a default installation of Funny video online has to offer, try out the <a target="_blank" href="http://fun.onlinerel.com/">Funny video online</a>

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder funny-video-online to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin Funny video online through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Add the widget to your sidebar from Appearance->Widgets and configure the widget options.
